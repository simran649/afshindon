Welcome to our Subject Enricment

THIS INITIATIVE TAKEN BY OUR SCHOOL WHICH IS CONDUCTED ON EVERY SATURDAY. THE ENRICHMENT PROGRAM IS CONDUCTED IN TWO BATCHES. ONE AND HALF HOUR FOR EACH. SUBJECT OFFERED MATHEMATICS, COMPUTER, SCIENCE, SOCIAL SCIENCE, MUSIC AND DANCE, SPORTS, ENGLISH, SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT OBJECTIVES : TO CREATE OPPORTUNITIES FOR THE DEVELOPMENT OF TALENTS AND PROVIDE SPECIAL TECHNIQUES AND RESOURCES.

Air Force School Hindan provides the students a platform on every Saturday to learn, explore and engage in different Subject Enrichment Activities as well as various Vocational training programmes. Subject Enrichment Activities promotes critical thinking and problem solving skills, improve students ability to concentrate and make learning more meaningful, valuable and rewarding
