Principal's Message

Welcome to our school! We are dedicated to fostering a nurturing and inclusive environment where every student can thrive and reach their full potential. Our dedicated staff and innovative programs ensure that our students receive a well-rounded education that prepares them for the future. Thank you for being a part of our community!.

The school building is podium type with basement parking and you all will be delighted to learn that the campus has a world class infrastructure with fully airconditioned, spacious, well-lit, colourful and interactive classrooms equipped with child friendly furniture and learner centric teaching aids, dance room, music room, art room, library, well-equipped computer labs, Amphitheatre and a Multi-Purpose Hall with hands-on activities keeping in mind the concept of experiential learning.

We are in the process to have strategically planned air-conditioned multi-purpose auditorium, indoor basketball court, badminton courts and a swimming pool. Outdoor cricket pitch, a skating rink and a football field are also in progress. To ensure the safety, security and well-being of the children in our care, we have explicit procedures and standards in place. These procedures will be periodically reviewed and updated to make sure that they reflect the most current safety and security technology and practices.

Each child is precious to us, safety and security of your ward is our topmost priority.
