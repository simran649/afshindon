school corridors

Our school corridors are not just pathways; they are spaces designed to inspire and uplift. The spacious and clean corridors, bathed in natural light, create a welcoming and positive atmosphere. The bright white walls and gleaming floors contribute to a sense of order and cleanliness, fostering a conducive learning environment.

A Caring Touch: Our School Medical Room

Our school medical room is a dedicated space designed to provide immediate healthcare assistance to students and staff. Equipped with essential medical supplies and staffed by trained medical personnel, it ensures the well-being of our school community.

Our school prioritizes the health and well-being of our students. Our on-campus medical facility is equipped with modern medical equipment and staffed by a team of experienced medical professionals. We provide immediate medical attention to students in case of emergencies, and also offer routine health check-ups and counseling services.

NDA Corner: Your Gateway to a Military Career

Our NDA Corner is a dedicated space designed to guide and support students who dream of joining the prestigious National Defence Academy (NDA). We offer comprehensive resources and expert guidance to help you achieve your goals.

A Harmonious Space: Our Music Room

Our music room is a haven for aspiring musicians, providing a dedicated space for practice, performance, and creative expression. Equipped with state-of-the-art instruments and sound systems, it offers a conducive environment for students to develop their musical talents.

Security

We understand that the safety and security of your child is paramount. That's why we've implemented a comprehensive security system, including over 300 CCTV cameras strategically placed throughout our school premises. This state-of-the-art surveillance system provides real-time monitoring of all areas, ensuring the well-being of every student.
