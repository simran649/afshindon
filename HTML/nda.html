Welcome to our NDA

The National Defence Academy (NDA) entry is available for aspirant who intend to join the Armed Forces (including Indian Air Force) after successful completion of 10+2. Through NDA entry, an aspirant can join the Flying, Ground Duty (Technical) and Ground duty (Non- Technical) branches, as per the following details
