Examination Process

Our assessment system evaluates students based on continuous performance, assignments, projects, and term-end examinations. Regular feedback and progress tracking ensure every student achieves their academic goals.

  • Primary Level: Activity-based assessments and periodic tests.
  • Secondary Level: Mid-term and annual examinations.
  • Senior Secondary Level: Board examination preparation and practicals.
