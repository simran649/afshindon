History of Our School

Air Force School Hindan was established in the year 1969 with a mission to provide holistic education to the children of Air Force personnel and civilians. Over the years, the school has grown significantly, adapting to the evolving educational needs and incorporating modern teaching methodologies.

Our school boasts a rich history of academic excellence, extracurricular achievements, and community involvement. We have consistently produced students who excel in various fields and contribute positively to society. Our alumni include notable personalities in diverse professions, reflecting the strong foundation laid by the school.

We are proud of our heritage and continue to uphold the values and traditions that have defined our institution. With a commitment to continuous improvement, we strive to create an environment where every student can thrive and achieve their fullest potential.

Air Force School Hindan, located in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, has a rich history of serving the educational needs of the community. Established in 1969, the school was founded by the Indian Air Force Educational and Cultural Society to cater to the children of Air Force personnel stationed in the National Capital Region.

Initially, the school started as a primary school but gradually expanded to include middle and senior secondary sections. Today, it offers education from LKG to Class XII and is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE)


Air Force School Hindan is known for its commitment to excellence in education, which was recognized in 2011 when it received ISO 9001-2008 certification
